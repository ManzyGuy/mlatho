Who We Are

Advancing Human Rights and Sustainable Development

Project Mlatho is an organization focusing on issues of democracy and governance, gender equality, human rights, and sustainable development. We are committed to ensuring that all female candidates elected to political office under our guidance leave their positions having made a positive and lasting impact on their communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in advancing human rights, sustainable development, and gender equality in Malawi.

Our Mission

To empower women in politics and contribute to the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals and the advancement of human rights throughout Malawi.

Our Objectives

Address Intersectional Challenges

Identify and address unique barriers based on socio-economic status, geographical location, and disability for women aspiring to participate in politics.

Advance Women's Political Participation

Actively promote and support advocacy efforts aimed at increasing women's political representation at all levels of government.

Create an Enabling Environment

Empower women in politics to effectively participate in initiatives related to SDGs through training and networking opportunities.

Foster Collaborative Partnerships

Share resources, expertise, and best practices with a diverse range of stakeholders including government and civil society organizations.

Project Mlatho

Empowering women leaders to drive sustainable development in their constituencies and communities.
