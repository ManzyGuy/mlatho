Our Goals

Address Intersectional Challenges

Identify and resolve the unique geographic, socio-economic, and ethnic barriers faced by women who aspire to or currently participate in politics in Malawi.

Promote Advocacy

Actively promote and support advocacy efforts designed to increase women's representation at all levels of government through policy changes.

Create an Enabling Environment

Empower women in politics to effectively implement UN Sustainable Development Goals via consistent training and continuous networking.

Foster Collaborative Partnerships

Establish strong strategic collaborations with government agencies, civil society organizations, and international partners to pool expertise.
