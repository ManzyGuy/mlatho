Our Team

Leadership & Governance

Mariah Kapindula

Executive Director

Mariah is an expert in democracy, governance, and political leadership. She holds a Master of Philosophy in Multidisciplinary Human Rights from the University of Pretoria. With over a decade of experience, her work has focused on gender equality, sustainable development, and international human rights frameworks, driving profound change alongside the Malawi Government, CSOs, and donor communities.

Mercy Bwanaisa

Deputy Executive Director / Monitoring & Evaluation

Mercy brings extensive knowledge in monitoring complex programs to ensure policies translate effectively on the ground. Her expertise enables Project Mlatho to continually adapt and ensure impactful deployment of resources for local constituencies.
